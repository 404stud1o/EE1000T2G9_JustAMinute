Features


BASIC FUNCTIONALITY

• 60 Seconds Counter
This product is initalised with a 60 seconds counter displayed on the central pyramid, and is operated by the 3 buzzer buttons on the top of each pyramids.

• The Buzzer Buttons
The buzzer will make a sound and pause the timer once any of the 3 buttons are pressed, the pressed button will also light up and system will lock the other buttons out so that the game cannot be continued by the other 2 judges.

KEY FEATURES

• Zero-Latency Timer Reset System
Once the time is up, the system can be re-initialised by the reset switch positioned at the back of the central pyramid.

• Plug & Play
This product is designed for plug-and-play!
The system is powered by a micro-USB connector, so you can simply use any USB power source
like a USB ports from a computer, an AC adapter plugged into the wall, or even just a powerbank!
