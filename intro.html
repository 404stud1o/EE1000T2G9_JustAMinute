CONTRACTOR OF THE PROJECT

We have a contract with the British Boardcast Corporation (BBC) to develop a prototype game quiz buzzer system for the popular radio show, Just A Minute, where participants must talk on a subject of engineering for 60 seconds without hesitation, repetition, or deviation.

The system allows 3 judges to press a buzzer if the contestant team detect a rule violation, or a wrong statement stopping the speaker.
