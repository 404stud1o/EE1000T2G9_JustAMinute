LIST OF COMPONENTS USED

  • 1 × Piezoelectric Buzzers
  • 6 × 5mm Red LEDs
  • 3 × SPST PTM Latching Switches
  • 1 × SPST PTB Momentary Switches
  • 1 × 555 Timer IC
  • 1 × 74HC393 Dual 4-bit Up Counter IC
  • 1 × 74HC00 Quad NAND Gate IC
  • 2 × CD4511 7-Segment Driver ICs
  • 1 × Double 7-Segment Displays Module
  • 1 × Capacitor
  • 20 × Resistors
  • 1 × USB Type Micro-B Female SMT Connector

OPERATING PARAMETERS

  • Current Flow: Direct Current (DC)
  • Operating Voltage: 5 Volts (V)
  • Minimum Operating Current: 500 Milliamperes (mA)
  • Maximum Operating Current: 2 Amperes (A)
  • Power Connection Method: USB Type B Micro

FINANCE

The maxmimum budget for this project is £30.00 GBP. Our team has minimized our budget by cutting down unnecessary components i.e. multiple buzzers, and also a fairly simple logical structure.

This resulted in a total development cost of £18.12, which is just over 60% of the maxmimum budget.
